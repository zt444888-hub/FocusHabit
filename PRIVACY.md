# Privacy Policy for FocusHabit

**Last updated: June 28, 2026**

FocusHabit does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All data created by you (habits, completion history, settings) is stored locally on your device and optionally synced across your devices via Apple iCloud.

No data is transmitted to any third-party servers.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at the email address registered with your Apple ID.
